About Vanakkam Annadata Foundation

It is a volunteer-driven initiative rooted in compassion and service. It provides daily, home-style meals to cancer patients and their families at Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Memorial Hospital in Raipur. These families, often from rural areas, face medical, emotional, and financial hardships. The foundation's meals symbolize not just nourishment but empathy, dignity, and human connection. Operating through a community kitchen, the initiative runs on public support and the spirit of seva (selfless service). It envisions a world where no patient or caregiver is left hungry or alone.
